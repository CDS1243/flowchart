Homepage

Entry point Users arrive at the homepage and choose to either sign up or log in if they already have an account

Sign Up

Users who are new to the platform will go through the registration process.

Profile Management

This section allows users to edit their profile information such as:
Bio
Interests
Photos
Preferences

View Matches

Users can see a list of their potential or confirmed matches based on their preferences and the app's matching algorithm.

Log In

This is the user’s entry point into the app. They provide their credentials (email/username and password) to access their account.

Verification Process

After signing up, users must verify their account, often via email or phone.

Swipe Left (No)

Users can swipe left if they are not interested in a match.

Swipe Right (Yes)

Users can swipe right to show interest.

Dashboard

Once profiles are completed, users access the dashboard, which serves as the app’s main hub for various features.

Search & Filter Options

This allows users to manually search for potential matches by applying various filters, such as:
Age. Location. Interests.

Complete Profile

After setting preferences, users finalize their profiles.

Set Preferences

Here, users can specify preferences for the type of people they want to be matched with (age, location, interests, etc.).

Create Profile

After setting preferences, users finalize their profiles.

Match Happens

If two users swipe right on each other, a match is made.

Match System - AI Suggestions

The AI-based matching system suggests potential matches based on the user's profile and preferences.

Upload Photos

Users upload their photos to enhance their profile’s visibility.

Notifications

Users receive alerts for important actions like: New messages.
Matches.
Event invitations.

WhatsApp-Like Chat

Chat with your match

Matched users can initiate a chat similar to WhatsApp, offering a comfortable communication experience.

App Features

  • Video Call Option:
    Face-to-face interactions for matched users.
  • Message Status: Sent, delivered, read indicators for transparent communication.
  • Notifications & Alerts: Get updates on messages, matches, and events.
  • Settings: Adjust various account and app settings.
  • Account Settings: Update personal info and profile details.
  • Manage Subscription: Manage or upgrade subscription plans, including premium options.
  • Upgrade to Premium: 2000 Naira per month for additional features.
  • Payment Gateway Integration: Choose between Paystack, Flutterwave, etc.
  • Payment Successful: Confirmation and next payment date notifications.
  • Cancel Subscription Option: Cancel subscriptions anytime.
  • Subscription Benefits: Premium members get extra features like profile visibility boost.
  • Privacy Settings: Control profile visibility and privacy options.
  • Discovery Mode: Join in-app events to enhance engagement.
  • Payment History: View payment history and transaction details.
  • Join In-App Events: Participate in special events for more interaction.
  • In-App Notifications: Stay updated with alerts for matches, messages, etc.
